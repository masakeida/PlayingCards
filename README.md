# PlayingCards
This is a hand generator for trick-taking games.
Deal 13 cards each for 4 players.

```
% ./PlayingCards
This is a hand generator for trick-taking games.

                        S: J 8 3
                        H: A K J 9 5 3
                        D: Q 9 2
                        C: Q

        S: 10 6                         S: K Q 9 4 2
        H: 8 7 4                        H: 2
        D: 10 7 6 4                     D: A J 8 3
        C: K 8 5 4                      C: A J 9

                        S: A 7 5
                        H: Q 10 6
                        D: K 5
                        C: 10 7 6 3 2

```
